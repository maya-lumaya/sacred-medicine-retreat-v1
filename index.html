The Three Sacred Medicines

Ancient allies, held with care.

Each medicine is offered in a guided group ceremony, with thorough preparation, support, and integration.

№ I
Nicotiana rustica

Rapéh

Amazonian snuff · group ceremony

Clears mental and emotional blockages, anchoring deep presence and a grounded connection to the Earth.

A sacred shamanic snuff medicine traditionally used by Indigenous tribes of the Amazon for ceremonial, medicinal, and spiritual purposes. The finely ground blend typically contains Nicotiana rustica (Mapacho) along with various ashes, barks, or medicinal plants, depending on the tribe and the intended energetic effect. Administered through the nose, Rapéh acts swiftly on the energetic body, clearing mental and emotional blockages while inducing a state of deep presence and inner stillness. It can help anchor prayers, release stagnant energy, and foster a grounded connection with the Earth and one's own spiritual path.

№ II
Banisteriopsis caapi · Psychotria viridis

Changa

Visionary plant blend · group ceremony

A smokable blend of DMT-containing plants and visionary herbs — a shorter, gentler doorway to vivid insight and emotional release.

Changa is a blend of DMT-containing plants combined with visionary herbs — often including the ayahuasca vine — which soften and gently extend the journey. Smoked in a ceremonial setting, it opens a shorter, more grounded visionary doorway than DMT alone: gentle in its onset, yet capable of remarkable depth. The experience often brings vivid imagery, emotional release, and a felt sense of reconnection — with yourself, with others, and with the living world. Many describe a profound sense of clarity and perspective as mental noise falls away and the heart opens. Held with care and intention, Changa can be a gentle yet powerful ally for insight, healing, and integration — a brief but meaningful glimpse beyond the ordinary mind.

№ III
Psilocybe cubensis

Psilocybin

Sacred mushroom · group ceremony

Opens the heart and quiets the mind, letting insight, emotion and vision surface for deep integration.

Psilocybin is the sacred compound found in certain mushrooms, used for millennia in healing and spiritual traditions for expanding awareness, and catalyzing deep inner transformation. In ceremonial settings, psilocybin softens mental conditioning, allowing emotions, insights, and visions to surface. It quiets the brain's default mode network, inviting profound connection with self, nature, and the greater whole. When held with care and intention, psilocybin becomes a wise ally for grounding mystical insight, healing old wounds, and reconnecting with purpose and presence.

Held by skilled facilitators · medical screening & health disclosure required before booking.
